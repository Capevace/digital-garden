## Alignment

$$
\\begin{split}
K(u) & = \int\_{-1}^{1} \frac{1}{2} ,du \\
& = \frac{1}{2} \int\_{-1}^{1} 1 ,du \\
& = \frac{1}{2} (1 - (-1))\\
& = 1\\
\\end{split}
$$
