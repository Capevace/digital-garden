## Alignment

$$
\begin{split}
K(u) & = \int_{-1}^{1} \frac{1}{2} \,du \\
& = \frac{1}{2} \int_{-1}^{1} 1 \,du \\
& = \frac{1}{2} (1 - (-1))\\
& = 1\\
\end{split}
$$
